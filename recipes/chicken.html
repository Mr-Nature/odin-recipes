Spiced Chicken laps

The picture above is a well spiced chicken

The chicken is well prepared, and spiced for the chicken recipe we are about to guide you on making. Make sure you follow the recipe stages one after the other, to arrive at the desired result in the image above.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Groundnut oil
  • Spices (Maggi, curry, theme, ginger, garlic)
  • Salt
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken parts carefully
  2. Put into a cooking pot
  3. Apply the spices into the washed chicken
  4. Apply a pinch of salt to it
  5. Cut Onions into the spiced up chicken
  6. Boil for 30 minutes or more (depending on the type of chicken in use)
  7. When fully cooked, put in a grilling pan
  8. Grill the cooked chicken until it's brown enough
  9. Enjoy your meal
